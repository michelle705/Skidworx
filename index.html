Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is forestry mulching?
Forestry mulching is a single-step land clearing method using a specialized machine with a rotating carbide-tooth cutting head that grinds trees, brush, and stumps directly into mulch on the ground. No hauling, no burn piles — one machine handles everything from start to finish.
How much does forestry mulching cost in Pasco County?
Costs typically range from $150–$400 per acre depending on vegetation density, tree diameter, and site accessibility. Every property is different — call (352) 206-6203 for a free on-site estimate with no obligation.
Do I need a burn permit for land clearing in Florida?
Not with SkidWorx. Our forestry mulching process grinds all vegetation into mulch on-site, completely eliminating the need for burn permits. This is one of the biggest advantages over traditional slash-and-burn clearing in Florida.
How long does land clearing take?
A typical 1–5 acre residential lot with moderate Florida brush can usually be cleared in one to two days. Larger or denser commercial properties may take longer. We'll give you an accurate timeline at your free estimate.
Will forestry mulching damage my topsoil?
No — it protects it. Our mulching head processes vegetation above ground and leaves the root systems and topsoil intact. The mulch layer left behind even helps suppress weeds and retain moisture, unlike traditional excavation methods.
Can you clear land after a storm or hurricane?
Yes. Storm recovery is one of our core services. We can rapidly mobilize across Pasco County to remove hazard trees, downed timber, and debris safely and efficiently. Call us immediately after a storm event for priority scheduling.
